My Photo Booth photo collection
and memories

Yeonjoo Noh

The prompt for this project was to create a webpage featuring a collection of 25 to 50 items using JSON. I decided to curate a collection showcasing the photo booth photos I’ve taken with my friends, which I’ve been collecting since my high school years. In the middle of the webpage, I created a collage featuring my face, which is visible from a distance. It is composed of close-up images of my friends' faces.
