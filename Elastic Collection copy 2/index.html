Since 2019, taking photos in a Photo Booth called 'Life 4 cuts' has emerged as a rising trend among Generation Z in Korea. It has become somewhat of a routine for them when hanging out with friends. To Generation Z, analog photos are seen as images that capture the mood of the day. They focus solely on themselves, regardless of how others view them. These cameras have gained popularity because they offer the opportunity to preserve special days and create good memories at a relatively cheap price.
